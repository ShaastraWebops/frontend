<h3> <span class =" small "> About the </span> <span class =" big "> Minute of silence </span> </h3>
<p> Every Anzac Day, we bow our heads and take a minute to remember. A minute to show our enormous gratitude for all those who’ve served, both past and present. It’s a time to think of all we’re blessed with, and those who fought to make it possible. Many of whom never made it home.This year the Minute of Silence means even more. When you call most of the cost will go to help veterans and their families. </p>
<h4> How your donation helps </h4>
<p> Money raised provides support in many ways: <br /> Medical and hospital expenses, mobile aids like wheelchairs, scooters and walking frames, household repairs and maintenance, grocery shopping, household bills. Home and hospital visits by volunteers also provide vital physical and emotional support. </p>

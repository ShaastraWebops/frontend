About Pledge-A-Book


Shaastra, IIT Madras takes up a notable social cause every year to contribute in giving back to the society. This year we have planned a vast social campaign, taking up the cause of children's education, the Pledge-A-Book campaign. We hope to do our bit to help and spread education, literacy, multilingualism and encourage a reading habit among underprivileged children. The books collected under this campaign would be used to set up libraries for underprivileged children in and around Chennai and in various places across the country..


The Pledge-A-Book campaign was flagged off by renowned science writer Mr. Anil Ananthaswamy. With the support of UNESCO and associations with notable NGOs such as Indian Development Foundation , Avanti, and charitable organisations we envision to take this campaign to a much larger scale.

How to support:

You can support the campaign by pledging your old books. We accept wide range of books. You can donate children's books, novels, JEE preparation materials. You can also support the campaign by pledging your engineering books. You can support the campaign by taking part in collection drives. We regularly organize collection drives inside the green campus of IIT Madras. You can also contribute to the campaign by dropping the books at IDF collection centers. At all IDF collection centers book box has been planted.

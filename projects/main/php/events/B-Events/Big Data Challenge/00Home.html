Introduction

 

Have you ever wondered how does your bank possibly know that the person buying clothes with your credit card isn’t you? How can a machine make an instant assessment of your tastes, health and age, just by looking at you? How can a video camera spot a potential terrorist just by the way he walks?

Well the answer lies in Big Data.

 

The volume of data in our world has been ever expanding, and analyzing big data has become a key footing of competition, underpinning new waves of productivity growth, innovation, and consumer surplus.

The Big Data Challenge tests you on how you go about using Big Data to address issues ranging from market investments to healthcare and come up with better and more beneficial decisions.

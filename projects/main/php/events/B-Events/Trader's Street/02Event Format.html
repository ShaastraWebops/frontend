Round 1: Online Trading

  1. In this round, participant will trade on mock Online Trading platform for 20 trading days. 
  2. Trading hours would be same as BSE/NSE trading hours(9:15 AM to 3:30 PM) from Monday to Friday.
  3. Money allocated is virtual and can only be used for our trading platform. Any profit or loss made during competition is also virtual and no claim can be made against it.
  4. Participant must make minimum of 15 trades during entire competition to be eligible to win the competition.
  5. Participant can only trade in Equity segment of BSE/NSE.
  6. Participant will be judged based on the net portfolio value(Virtual cash + Stock value).
  7. Trading duration dates will be declared soon.

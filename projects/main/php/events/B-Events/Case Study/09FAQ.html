Frequently Asked Questions

 

1. What is the maximum size of team ?

  • A team can consist of 2 team members at maximum.

 

2. Do all members need to be from the same college ?

  • No, team members can be from different colleges.

 

3. Do I need to register online ?

  • No ! The event requires on-spot registration and no online registration is needed. We recommend you signup to the website and subscribe for updates.

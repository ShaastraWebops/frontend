Frequently Asked Questions

1. What will be the size of the team?

  • The size of the team is restricted from 3 - 5 members. Minimum 3 required.


2. Do all the members need to be from the same college?

  • YES. All team members have to be from the same institute


3. I am not an M.B.A student . Can I participate in the event?

  • Yes.The event is not specific to M.B.A students only. Anyone satisfying the eligibility criteria can participate in the event.

 

4. Can a participant be in two teams ?

  • No, Each participant can only be a part of 1 team.

 

5. What is the eligibility criterion ?

  • You will have to present a valid ID from a recognized educational institute.

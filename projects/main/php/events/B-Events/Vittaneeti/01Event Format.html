Event Format

General Rules

The event is open  to  Bachelor as  well as Post graduate colleges across India. 

It is a team event .A team comprises of Minimum of two member , Maximum of three. 

Team has to register themselves at the  b-event  Column of the  Shaastra website.

The member  of the team should be from the  same Institute .

There is no entry fee

link for trading platform will be send to the primary Email -id of the Team Leader.

Only one email id will be used as for correspondence, However the Official id of the Team Member are Required .

Continuous update regarding the market functioning ,investment tips will be posted to our facebook page. 

ROUND 1 

There would be Online Quiz that would be Conducted on 10th nov 2014 at 22:30 PM. The quiz will check the participant knowledge from various domain related to finance ,Trading ,latest business affairs.

The winners wll be intimated  through the email .Result will  be published in the  shaastra website and facebook page.

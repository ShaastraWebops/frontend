Introduction


You don't have to be genius or a visionary, or even a college graduate for that matter to be successful. You just need framework and a dream. - Michael Dell, CEO Dell

Do you have what it takes to lead a multimillion dollar corporation?

       This January, be where the nation's shrewdest minds will be, Vittaneeti. Put yourself in the shoes of a CFO, whether acquiring a million dollar business or saving your beloved from bankruptcy. Solve it, LIKE A BOSS, quite literally.

 

Event Format

Round 1 - Premininary online round

It will be an online quiz testing your finance acumen on www.dare2compete.com. Only the teams that pass this will be allowed to continue on.

Round 2 - Online Case Study

This round will involve you and your group cracking a real-life case study from top multinational firms testing your financial prowess and acumen.

Round 3 - Finale

This will be on-campus event. The participants will present their solutions to the judges panel from the multinational firm.

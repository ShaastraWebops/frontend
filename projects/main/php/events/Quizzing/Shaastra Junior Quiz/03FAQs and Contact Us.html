1. What is the age restriction for participation, if any?
A)There is no age restriction, and we encourage every student to give it their best shot. The level of the questions is mostly aimed at 10th, 11th and 12th standard students.
2. What topics does the quiz cover?
A)The quiz will be general in nature, covering everything under the sun. Slightly more importance will be given to the sci-tech genre, and teams can expect a sizable amount of questions from here.
3. How do I register for this event?
A)The quiz follows an on spot registration pattern, and teams can just show up at the venue with their school id. The Shaastra facebook page will have venue details and timings.
4. My city regional is over, but I REALLY want to participate. What do I do?
A)TRAVEL. Travel to your nearest city, and give it a shot. Passion, determination and intelligence is what separates a winner from the pack.

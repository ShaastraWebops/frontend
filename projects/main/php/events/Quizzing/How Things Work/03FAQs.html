Q1 : What should the size of the team be?
Ans : A team can have 3 or less members. If you can't find a teammate, come to the venue - we'll try to find one for you!

Q2 : Do all members need to be from the same college?
Ans : No, team members can be from different colleges.

Q3: I've never quizzed before. Is attending a good idea?
Ans: Absolutely. The How Things Work quiz is always won by the team that can think creatively on the spot and figure out how a certain device works. No prior knowledge of the device required. Just, well, creative thinking.

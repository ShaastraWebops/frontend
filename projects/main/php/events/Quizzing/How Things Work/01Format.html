Basics

 Round 1: On-spot Prelims
The Prelims will be written, consisting of 20 questions. You should have Shaastra Passport(which will be available at the Hospitality Desk) to participate in the On-spot Prelims.
Round 2: Finals
The top five teams from the on-spot prelims (along with the team from Round 0) will make it to the finals which will consist of a number of standard and non-standard rounds

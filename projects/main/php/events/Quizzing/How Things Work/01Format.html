Stage 1: On-spot Prelims

The prelims will be written round, consisting of around 25 questions. You should have a Shaastra Passport (which will be available at the Hospitality Desks) to participate in the on-spot prelims.

Stage 2: Finals

The top 8 teams from the on-spot prelims will make it to the finals which will consist of multiple rounds, both written and oral.

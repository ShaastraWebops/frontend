Round 1: On-spot Prelims
The prelims will be written, consisting of around 25 questions. You should have a Shaastra Passport (which will be available at the Hospitality Desk) to participate in the on-spot prelims. No pre-registration required. Just show up. Students from IITM don't need a Passport - just their ID card.
Round 2: Finals
The top 8 teams from the on-spot prelims will make it to the finals which will consist of multiple rounds, both written and oral.

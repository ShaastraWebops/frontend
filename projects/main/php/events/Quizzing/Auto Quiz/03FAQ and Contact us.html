Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What will be the size of the team?
A. The maximum size of the team is restricted to 2 members.

Q. Do all members need to be from the same college?
A. No, team members can be from different colleges.

Q. What topics will the quiz cover?
A. The quiz will cover everything related to automobiles. Questions will be based on exotic & vintage cars, car commercials, motorsports, technical stuffs etc.

Q. Will there be a lot of technical questions?
A. There will be a few questions which are technical, but nothing that the normal auto enthusiast would have a problem with.

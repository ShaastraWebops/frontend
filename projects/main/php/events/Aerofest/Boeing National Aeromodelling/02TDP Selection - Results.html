Not Selected in Round 1

All those who have not been selected in the first round, are eligible for the 2nd round of TDP Selections.

You are required to change certain aspects of your TDP , making it better in terms of the content given in the abstract. Please mention more details about the design process like how did you decide on the dimensions , and some more information about your plane. How is it different from a conventional plane, and how is it suitable for the given Problem Statement.

Please take this in a positive manner, and make sure your TDP gets better and prove your mettle.

Deadline for Submission of TDP in the 2nd Round: 18th December 2015, 11:59 pm.

Please upload your new TDP on the website and mail the new TDP to boeing_aero@shaastra.org, in the same thread as you have mailed the earlier TDP.

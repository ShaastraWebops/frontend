Frequently Asked Questions

 

1. What is Airshow ?

  • It is a RC flying pro-show which includes performances by national and international flyers.

 

2. Where is it held ?

  • Airshow will be held during Shaastra at IIT Madras' Chemplast Cricket Ground

 

3. Are there any restrictions to the entry of the show - to view ?

  • No, The show is open for all.

 

4. Do I need to purchase tickets for the entry ?

  • No, The show is entirely free. You may walk in at any time.

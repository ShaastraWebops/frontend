Introduction 

One of the biggest RC flying shows in the country, with planes and helicopters all twisting and twirling and fliers using the sky as the canvas for their masterpieces.

This year, we bring to Chennai, some of world's best aeromodellers. Wolfgang Krahofer, Gernot Bruckmann from Austria and 14 years old Jase Dussia from USA are sure to amaze all with their 3d extreme aircraft flying. Watch the night, lit up with night flying and pyrotechnics.  We also bring the aeromodellers in and around Chennai with their best show.

Shaastra Air Show never fails in leaving its spectators in Awe!

Introduction

One of the best RC flying shows in the country, with planes and helicopters all twisting and twirling, and fliers using the sky as the canvas for their masterpieces.

This year, we bring to Chennai, some of world's best aeromodellers. Wolfgang Krahofer and Marian Mader are sure to amaze all with their 3d extreme aircraft flying and Gunther Gold will be back to make his heli groove to his tunes with his 3d extreme helicopter flying. Watch the night, lit up with night flying and pyrotechnics.  We also bring the aeromodellers in and around Chennai with their best show including Control Line flying and Ornithopters..

This Shaastra 2015, the Show is in the Air!

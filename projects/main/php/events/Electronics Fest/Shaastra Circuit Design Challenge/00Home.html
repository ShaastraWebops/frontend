Event Format

Round 1 - Prelims (TDP Submission)

In this round, all the participating teams will need to submit a Team Description Paper (TDP). You will have to provide your complete team details and also give feasible solutions to the problem statements provided.

Round 2 - Final Round

Shortlisted teams from the first round will be invited to IIT Madras during Shaastra 2015 to present their solutions and ideas.

Final round will comprise of a presentation and also demonstration of the models made by each team.

Some of the greatest minds from the Circuits and Systems Community will judge your design, evaluating its feasibility and novelty.

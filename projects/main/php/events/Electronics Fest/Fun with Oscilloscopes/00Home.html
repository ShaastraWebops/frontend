Fun with Oscilloscopes

Learn the basics of using an oscilloscope in a fun manner by drawing images on an oscilloscope screen. You must have heard that oscilloscopes are instruments used to measure waveforms. But, did you ever imagine that they could be used as a tools for drawing?

Break the bounds of your imagination and draw the best image on the oscilloscope.  No participant leaves empty handed at the end of the day as all participants get to take home their very own oscilloscope.

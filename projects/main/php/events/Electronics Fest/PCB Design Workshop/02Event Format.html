In this workshop we will have a hands on session on making a home made PCB. We will kick start with giving brief introduction to PCB and  with basics of  EAGLE (a PCB design  software) and give a detailed  process of  designing a PCB.  After that participants  will  prepare their own PCB which they can take home.

It will be a 2 - 3 hours workshop conducted during day 4 ( 6th January 2015 ) of Shaastra.

All the required resources for making PCB will be provided to participants during workshop.

It will be preferred if the participants bring laptops with EAGLE installed in them.

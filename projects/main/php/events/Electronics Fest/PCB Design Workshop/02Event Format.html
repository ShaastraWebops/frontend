In this workshop we will have hands on session on making a home made PCB. we will kick start with giving brief introduction to PCB and  with basics of  EAGLE (a PCB design  software) and give a detailed  process of  designing a PCB.  After that participants  will  prepare their own PCB which can take to their home.

It was a 2 - 3 hours workshop conducted during shaastra.

All the required resources for making PCB will be provided to participants during workshop.

Better to bring laptops with EAGLE installed in it

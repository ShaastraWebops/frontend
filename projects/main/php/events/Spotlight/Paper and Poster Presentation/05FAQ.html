QUERIES

Q. Can I participate in this event individually?
A.  Yes, you can participate individually or have a team of maximum 4 members.

Q. Does the team have to be from one college?
A.  No, you can form a cross college team - as long as all the team members can provide a valid college identification.

Q. Can I submit more than one abstract?
A.  No, every participant is allowed to submit only one abstract each. 

Q. I don't have  research paper. Can I participate?
A.  Yes, you can still present your project provided it is your original work. 
 

Q. Can I present others' work?
A.  NO, plagiarized work is strictly prohibited.

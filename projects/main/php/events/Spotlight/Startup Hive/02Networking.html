One to One Mentorship

We understand that each one of you is different and therefore our emphasis will be on providing a customised mentoring session. Based on your requirements, experts will be invited for a 30 minute discussion.

The Informal Connect

All work aside, the people that we hope meet at the Startup Hive will hopefully form useful business connections for life. To that end, all the participating entrepreneurs, investors, and mentors shall be invited to a special networking dinner.

The Connections Wall

A simple yet powerful way to enhance networking between the various people present- be it between startups, or mentor-startup, it is time-tested to be the most effective way of carrying this out.

Pecha Kucha Talks
Japanese for "Chit-Chat" , they involve short time periods where startups tell everyone else present what they do.

What?

StartupHive is an event that envisions to bring together talented people from various facets of the start-up scene so that they can engage, network and grow together. From entrepreneurs to investors, designers to marketers - the brightest prospects and stalwarts of the field alike will be there and we hope to see you there as well.

Lectures

We will be organising lectures on extremely relevant topics by experts sourced from pioneers in the field of business & entrepreneurship, tapping into the very best of the network.

Topics that shall be explored include -

  • Assessing your business plan like a venture capitalist

  • Fundraising - a stage wise guide

  • Establishing yourself as a new player in the market

  • Expanding your start-up: hiring and growth

One to One Mentorship

We understand that each one of you is different and therefore our emphasis will be on providing a customised mentoring session. Based on your requirements, experts will be invited for a 30 minute discussion.

The Informal Connect

All work aside, the people that we hope meet at the Startup Hive will hopefully form useful business connections for life. To that end, all the participating entrepreneurs, investors, and mentors shall be invited to a special networking dinner.

When?

The event is scheduled for 2nd January 2015.

Why?

Of the many reasons, here are just a few -

  • One to one mentoring session with experts in fields of your choice. An early confirmation in this regard would help us understand your requirement and search our extensive network and tap into varied associations to find a mentor that caters specifically to your needs

  • Networking with a plethora of investors, successful entrepreneurs and expert lecturers in various fields.

What?

 

"Your time is limited, so don't waste it living someone else's life." - Steve Jobs

 

With that opening line, our nation stands at the brink of an entrepreneurial revolution. One that will be propelled by creative go-getters and the disruptive innovation they unleash. IIT Madras and Shaastra would like to take the first step forward in empowering this generation to design their own dreams.

 

Setting up the backdrop of this confluence to address some of the common concerns about entrepreneurship as a future path, we hope to give back the reins of change to each person running the race. Hear firsthand the experiences of seasoned entrepreneurs and corporate moguls from diverse fields.
 

Keynote Address: Ranga Kanapathy, PayPal India

 

Ranga Kanapathy is the Director of Data Technology at PayPal India and leads Global Data Operations, Quality and Release Management teams. He has strong expertise in establishing cost-effective and company-wide visions, and leading geographically dispersed and multi-cultural organizations. Before taking up this role, he led Risk Management & Global Operations Product Development teams and played an instrumental role in setting up Architecture, Infrastructure, Security Engineering, Asia-Pac Site Operations, and Quality Assurance teams in India. 
Prior to PayPal, Ranga was an entrepreneur at Snobol Computers Pvt. Ltd., where he focused on the sales and marketing of software and hardware products. He moved on to work with multinational companies including CSC, Visa, Mattel, Esurance, and Fisher Price. Over his career spanning 20+ years, he worked across Financial, Credit Cards, Insurance, Retail, and Manufacturing domains. Ranga is a member of TiE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) and PMI (Project Management Institute).

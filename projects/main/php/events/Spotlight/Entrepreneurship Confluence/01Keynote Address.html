Keynote Address

 

Ranga Kanapathy

Ranga Kanapathy is the Director of Data Technology at PayPal India and leads Global Data Operations, Quality and Release Management teams. He has strong expertise in establishing cost-effective and company-wide visions, and leading geographically dispersed and multi-cultural organizations.

Before taking up this role, he led Risk Management & Global Operations Product Development teams and played an instrumental role in setting up Architecture, Infrastructure, Security Engineering, Asia-Pac Site Operations, and Quality Assurance teams in India.

Prior to PayPal, Ranga was an entrepreneur at Snobol Computers Pvt. Ltd., where he focused on the sales and marketing of software and hardware products. He moved on to work with multinational companies including CSC, Visa, Mattel, Esurance, and Fisher Price. Over his career spanning 20+ years, he worked across Financial, Credit Cards, Insurance, Retail, and Manufacturing domains.

Ranga is a member of TiE (The Indus Entrepreneurs) and PMI (Project Management Institute) and is a strong proponent of a Technology-centric culture that drives today’s young technologists. He is a people-centric person and strongly believes that the success of any team depends on people getting more connected.

Ranga holds B.Com degree from Viveka PU College, ICWA from Institute of Cost and Work Accountants of India, MBA in Computer Applications from Anna University, and MBA in Information technology from Uptron ACL. Ranga is a drummer and loves playing cricket. He lives in Chennai with his wife and two sons.

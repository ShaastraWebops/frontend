Welcome! Follow these simple steps to register for the Symposium.

 

  1. Visit the Shaastra login page here and register yourself for the festival
  2. On the dashboard, in the events tab, choose "Symposium" as the event in the dropdown menu and upload the answers to ONE of the problem statements (either Health Technology or Health Policy) on the dashboard. The problem statements for both tracks can be found below.
  3. Click "Save Submission"

Note:

  • A selected participant would be intimated by mail. The participant would be required to pay a nominal registration fee and can then proceed to book travel tickets.
     
  • Registration cost structure: 
    Local Chennai participants       Rs. 500
    Indian (non-Chennai) participants      Rs. 750 
    Foreign participants       Rs. 4800
  • The participant has to only bear the cost of travel. Local accommodation and food will be provided by us.
  • The registration fee would be transferred through online banking to the Shaastra account. Selected participants would be instructed as to how to go about the process.
  • Have queries? Drop us a line at symposium@shaastra.org

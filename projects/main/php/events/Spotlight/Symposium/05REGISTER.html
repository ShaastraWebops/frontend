Welcome! Registration involves two short stages:

Stage 1:

  • The Symposium consists of 2 tracks - Health Technology and Health Policy.
  • An interested participant would have to pick ONE of the two and correspondingly answer that track's problem statement.
  • Problem Statements:
    The problem statement for Health Technology can be found here.
    The problem statement for Health Policy can be found here.
  • The answers to the problem statements must be emailed to symposium@shaastra.org in a pdf format BEFORE 20th October 2014.
  • The results of the same would be announced in the first week of November 2014.

 

Stage 2:

Once selected, the participant would be required to pay a nominal registration fee (through online banking) and then can proceed to book travel tickets.

Registration cost structure: 

Local Chennai participants  Rs. 500
Indian (non-Chennai) participants Rs. 750
Foreign participants  Rs. 4800

 

Note:

  • A selected participant would be intimated by mail.
  • The participant has to only bear the cost of travel. Local accommodation and food will be provided by us.
  • The registration fee would be transferred through online banking to the Shaastra account. Selected participants would be instructed as to how to go about the process.
  • Have queries? Drop us a line at symposium@shaastra.org

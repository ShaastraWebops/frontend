WHY SYMPOSIUM?

 

The IIT Madras International Symposium offers something for everyone.

It is held during Shaastra, IIT Madras' annual technical festival. It is the first ISO 9001:2008 certified student-organized technical festival. It attempts to include multiple perspectives to promote a more inclusive approach to technology and policy making.

Shaastra 2014 saw a footfall of around 25,000 visitors from all across the country. Our stages were graced by some of the biggest names in the industry and academia as well as the brightest young minds from across the country. Shaastra has evolved to being more than just a technical festival - it is today India's foremost techno-entertainment and management festival.

 

The IIT Madras International Symposium is set to be Shaastra's biggest event this year.

The highlights of this year's symposium include a stellar line-up of speakers and mentors from the industry, a case study defined by an industry partner and cash prizes amounting to Rs. 60,000. With two key-note lectures and six mentoring sessions in place, this symposium promises to truly broaden one's perspective.

We are calling participation from all around the world, and armed with the knowledge of the healthcare scenario of their country, these international students would bring their own unique idea of healthcare to the table.

Along with the immense cultural exchange, participants would be exposed to different ideas of what healthcare means in different places. There would be a field trip organised in association with our partner organisation to sensitise the participants towards the issues that rural India faces with respect to even basic healthcare.

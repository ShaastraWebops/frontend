Frequently Asked Questions

 

1. What is the IITMIS? 
The IITMIS stands for Indian Institute of Technolgy Madras International Symposium. It is a platform to discuss, debate and ideate on issues concerning the public at large. This year, we focus on the Indian Healthcare scenario and would attempt to come up with possible ways to improve it in three broad areas - the infusement of new technologies, bridging the large disparity and modifying policies. 
 
2. When and where is the IITMIS going to be held? 
The IITMIS will be held during the annual technical festival of IIT Madras, Shaastra, in the first week of January, 2015, in the IIT Madras campus. It would continue for a period of 4 days. 
 
3. What is the criterion for eligibility? 
The participants must be enrolled in an undergraduate or post-graduate course in a recognised university.
 
4. What are the benefits of participating in the IITMIS?
The Symposium gives you an opportunity to work with some of the brightest minds from around the world on a problem which is of paramount concern. Mentoring sessions, ice-breaking and networking sessions, field trips and a case study competition await the participants of the symposium. 
 
5. What is the topic for The Symposium? 
Healthcare 
 
6. What is the selection procedure for The Symposium?
There will be an online preliminary selection round from which finalists will be short-listed.
 
7. Could you name some partnering organizations from the years gone by? 
Some of the partners include: World Bank Urban Strategy, TERI, FICCI, IFMR, Agastya International Foundation.

 

8. Would we have to pay a registration fee? 

Yes, all participants of the IITMIS would have to pay a nominal fee which would cover all basic expenses including accommodation, food and travel. The participants would have to fund their own travel to and from the IIT Madras campus and their home universities. 

 

9. What about accommodation and food? 

The IITMIS provides accommodation and food to all paticipants of the symposium.

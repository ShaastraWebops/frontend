SCHEDULE

Note:The detailed schedule for the 4 days of the symposium would be released closer to the date. Selected participants would be informed of the same through mail. 

Day 1 - Welcome!

The first day of the symposium would set the tone for the next four days at IIT Madras. Our prominent keynote speakers would be speaking on our two tracks: 

  1. Technological innovations in Healthcare and the influence of big data, as well as the ill-effects of excessive technology in healthcare
  2. Policies currently prevalent in the Indian healthcare industry and important issues like FDI in healthcare and the intellectual property rights (IPR) issue. Issues involving gender, social and economic inequality shall also be dealt with. 

Followed by a long day in the auditorium, an ice-breaker cum dinner gathering would facilitate further interaction between our international and Indian students and allow the participants to mingle with some of the best from industry.

The participants would be split into teams to help them work on the case study, the solution to which would have to be presented to a panel of judges on Day 4. 

Day 2 - Mentor and be mentored!

Participants interested in each track would have a chance to pick the brains of our mentors - coming from various fields in Healthcare. A stimulating environment to facilitate intense discusson and debate on the various issues at hand would be fostered. 

Day 3 - Time to see what you've read!

Field trips organised with assistance from our partner organisations would aim to sensitise the participants towards the problems faced by the rural poor with regards to healthcare – accessibility, quality and frequency of hospital visits among others. 

The participants would return and discuss their observations with the mentors for an even better understanding. 

Day 4 - Put your knowledge to the test!

The teams would be asked to present their solution and present it to the mentors and partner organizations. 

Big prizes up for grabs!

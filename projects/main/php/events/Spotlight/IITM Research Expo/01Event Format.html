Selected Students must present a poster following the specifications given:

  • Title: Project title.
  • Authors: Your name, faculty advisor’s name, names of collaborators, and department.
  • Purpose (or Objectives or Introduction).
  • Methods.
  • Results or Findings.
  • Discussion.
  • Summary/conclusions.
  • Acknowledgments: include any sources of funding your received.

Also note:

  • Design and print a large-scale poster (required dimensions: 36” high, 48” wide)
  • Font size should be 18 minimum and 44 maximum.
  • Use only 2 or 3 colors in the poster for fonts.
  • Every team given at maximum of 20 minutes.
  • Participants will be judged by a panel of Esteemed judges who are pioneers in the feild of research.

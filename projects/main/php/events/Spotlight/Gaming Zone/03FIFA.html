Event format:-

  • Competition method :1 vs. 1 challenge
  • Round 1 :
    4 minutes half and 3 minutes given to players to choose their team and formations,etc. Winner moves to the next round.
  • Round 2 :
    Round 2 onwards time increases by 2 min every round (Total no. of rounds depend on the number of registered participants).
  • Controllers will be provided to players.

 

Rules & Regulations:-

  • Game Version: FIFA 14/15. Game mode : “KICK OFF”
  • Controllers will be provided for this game.., Personal controllers of the player will  be allowed.However, the player will be given a chance to set the controls as per his preferences.
  • Tournament Format : 1 v 1 (Player Vs Player)
  • The duration of each game depends on the stage of the tournament.

         For the initial rounds it will be 4 minutes a half. While it would be 5 minutes a half for the quarter­finals and 6 minutes for  the  semi­final and final rounds.This information will be announced before the start of each match.

  • Two minutes will be allotted to edit line­up's and configure controls.
  • Winning a match : The player with the most number of goals wins the games.
  • The team can be changed at any stage of the competition.
  • Classic XI and World X1 are not allowed.
  • Custom formations are allowed but there is to be NO man marking tactics applied.
  • Each player will be allowed a maximum of three pauses during the game of duration 30 seconds each. An additional one minute pause will be given when half­time is reached. These pauses can be used by a player to access team management. However, a player can only pause the game if he's having the ball possession.
  • Coin must be tossed to decide which player gets to choose the home or away side.
  • In case of a problem at the start of the match, such as confusing squad kits for the players, the match will be restarted if both the players agree.
  • Recent online updates in squads will not be done.
  • If a match result is disputed, the losing player must contact a volunteer immediately.
  • No coaching allowed (i.e, people in the audience may not advise a player while playing)


 

General Game Settings :

  • Injuries: On
  • Offsides: On
  • Bookings: On
  • Subs on bench: 7
  • Condition: Dry/Clear
  • Time/Score display: On
  • Camera: Tele
  • Game Speed : Norm

 

Disconnections:

Any disconnection of the connection between match players due to System, Network, PC, and/or Power problems/issues.

  • Intentional: Upon judgment by the referee, any offending player will be charged with a loss by forfeit.
  • Unintentional: If the disconnection is deemed to be unintentional by the referee, the match will be restarted. If any player does not agree to a match restart, that player will lose by default.

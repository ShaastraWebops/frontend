Event Format :

Problem statements are out for this event and the participants (team of max 3 members) have to make a presentation on any one of the problem statement and are requested to mail it to idp@shaastra.org by 12:00 pm, 05-01-2015* and this presentation can also be uploaded in your Shaastra web profile.

Finalists will be chosen on the basis of the presentation submitted and These finalists will have the golden opportunity of presenting their presentation to the shaastra 2015 audience and a group of esteemed judges on the final day of shaastra 2015 i.e (06-01-2015)*.

*The deadline of app submisson and final round are subjected to change, Please check this page regularly for updates.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1.Can students from different institutes register together?   Yes. Students from different institutes can form teams together. Also, students from any stream can participate in this event.

 

 2.Can we submit an entry of our own Problem statement?        No. Submissions for the problem statements stated in this site will only be accepted for evaluation.

     

3.What are the rules for eligibility?
You are eligible as long as you are a registered student of any school or college. Everyone is welcome to participate, but prizes will be given only to students.

 

4.How do I submit the entry?

You must login to the Shaastra userportal and register yourself for this  event. You  have to submit your solution there after the registration process.

Bust out your thinking caps and let the creative genius in you take the center stage. Yes, Project X is back! And this time with much more thought provoking problem statements, extravaganza and a bag full of goodies!

Project X is a 2-day event in Shaastra which mainly focuses on creativity and craftsmanship. The participants will be given a fresh problem statement each day to build a model of a real world entity for a stipulated time of two hours using ice cream sticks, straws, glue and other household items. With its reputation as the largest participated event in Shaastra, behold the unpredictable!

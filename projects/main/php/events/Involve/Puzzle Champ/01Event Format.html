Prelims:Two independent preliminary rounds will be held on two separate days. Each round will be of 3 hours and will have 15 to 20 written puzzles. Based on the performance, the top 4 teams from each round will be selected to the finals.
 

Finals:The shortlisted teams will be given 3 to 6 big hands-on puzzles followed by a lot of short puzzles to solve within 3 hours.

WCA Registration

After registering in Shaastra portal,Please click on this link: WCA registration, to enroll yourself in the competition. 

World Cube Association (WCA) wants all participants to register in their website also, so click on the link above and do register yourself. 

If you are a first-timer, click on the "Iam new!" button after clicking on the above link.

If you have any queries or doubts in this process,

Please contact us at shaastra_cube_open@shaastra.org or personally call us. This registration is very important.

 

Click on the following link to see the list of registered participants : 

List of Registered Participants.

 

If you have registered once, please wait until we accept it. Give us some time to go through your details. So don't register multiple times. 

Also, your name should consist of two words, don't include initials. This is very essential, if you are a first- timer.

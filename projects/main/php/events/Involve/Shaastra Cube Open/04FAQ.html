Frequently Asked Questions

Q3  : Is there any age limit?
A: No, as long as you know how to solve a cube, no problems.

 

Q4: When and where the event is going o be held? 

A: The event is gonna be held at IIT Madras, during the Shaastra from 3rd to 6th January.
 

Note :   Shaastra ID is compulsory to participate in Cube open.

Event Format

 

Shaastra Cube Open gives a platform for cubers to showcase their speed cubing talents,thus providing them a great chance of breaking several records. Following are the list of categories in which participants can showcase their talent.

Basic categories:

  • 2x2 Speed solving
  • 3x3 Speed solving
  • 4x4 Speed solving

Other categories : 

  • 3x3 Single Handed
  • 3x3 Blindfolded
  • 3x3 Multiple Blindfolded
  • Pyraminx
  • Square-1
  • Skewb

Please read through the regulations and rules put up by the World Cube Association (WCA), before coming to the event.

Please refer to the Schedule of the event for two days.

NOTE: Please note that the Schedule may change.

How to Apply

1. For this, you will need :

  • A team of 3-4 enthusiastic team-mates
  • 5 minute youtube video of yourself pitching your idea to us
  • A presentation explaining the same idea
  • 1 slide in the presentation telling us about the budget required by you, and the break-up at different series of fundings.

Startup ideas falling under one of the below themes of national importance will be preferred :

  • Education
  • Healthcare
  • Water and Sanitation
  • Waste management
  • Energy
  • Agriculture
  • Transportation and environment
  • Affordable housing
  • Assistive technology

2. The selected entries will be given an office space in CFI, and asked to present an A2 sized poster to industry experts and alumni attending the CFI Open House. They will also be entitled to their demand in the first round of funding, which we call ‘seed funding’, in return for a list of deliverables which they are expected to show before the next round of funding. They are eligible for funding of 5-20% of their total proposed budget on this day.

3. There are subsequent levels of funding, when they are eligible to ask for bigger part of their total budget over the next few months. For being eligible for the next round of funding, they must have fulfilled the deliverables promised in the previous round, and have plans to work on their project further.

Type of funding Approximate Date Percentage of total budget
Series A 4th week of September ’14 10-15 %
Series B 1st week of November ‘14 30-40%

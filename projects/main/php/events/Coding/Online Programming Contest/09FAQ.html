Frequently Asked Questions

 

1. What is the procedure for registration?

  • You can register on the website and register to the event. Additionally, teams should register on codechef to submit solutions. Teams completing both the registrations alone will be eligible for prizes.

 

2. Is this a team event? Is single participation allowed ?

  • Yes, this is a team event. Team size of upto 3 members is allowed. Though not recommended, you are still allowed to participate individually.
     

3. Can I do something to prepare for this contest?

  • Yes, this falls under the category of events called "Competitive programming". Besides the ones mentioned in the resources tab, there are a number of tutorials available on the internet.

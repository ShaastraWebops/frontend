Event Format

 

Prelims (Online):  The teams will need to answer a set of questions which will be posted online, based on which they will be shortlisted and invited to IIT Madras for the final round.

Prelims (Offline): The teams will need to answer a written test during Shaastra 2015. Based on their performance, teams will be shortlisted for the final round.

Finals: All the teams shortlisted from both the online and the offline round will have to debug few buggy codes and write the programs. Teams will be judged on their accuracy as well as their efficiency.

 

 

The offline prelims round and the final round will be held during Shaastra 2015 at IIT Madras. 

You can enter the finals by passing either the online prelims or the offline prelims. You need not attend both.

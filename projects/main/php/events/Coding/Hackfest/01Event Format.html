EVENT FORMAT

 

Registrations are conducted on the basis of a questionnaire which will be released soon. There will be no on-spot registration.

Round 1: Prelims(Questionnaire)

In this round, the participants are required to submit answers to a questionnaire seeking just to test the basic familiarity with Linux and ways to operate it. Around 80 will be selected for attending the event.

Round 2: Hacknights

Invited participants will be able to participate in 3 nights of hacking,aided by mentors

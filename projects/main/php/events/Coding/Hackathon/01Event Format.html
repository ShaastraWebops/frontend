Event Format

Prelims:

    The team needs to send a Video of the working prototype along with a Report in
the pdf format and the Source code. The shortlisted teams will be invited to Shaastra for the finals.

 

Finals:

    The shortlisted teams will be given a new Problem Statement about a week before
Shaastra. The teams need to give a Presentation on 5th january about their prototype during Shaastra.

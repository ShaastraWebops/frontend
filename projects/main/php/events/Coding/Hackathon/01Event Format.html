Event Format

Prelims:

    The team needs to send a video of the working prototype tackling the Prelims problem statement, along with a report (in pdf format) and the source code. The shortlisted teams will be invited to Shaastra for the finals.

 

Finals:

    The shortlisted teams will be given a new problem statement, approximately a week before
Shaastra. The teams, with support from the experienced mentors provided to them, would need to give a presentation of their solution on 5th January, 2015 at Shaastra.

Event Format

    The Problem Statement is very generic. You will be given a real world domain in which you 
have to prepare a working prototype which has some real world application and related to the 
given domain.


    For example the domain can be like E-Commerce and you can design prototypes such as 
Payment gateway or Fraud detector or any other things which relate to the given area.

 

Prelims:

    The team needs to send a Video of the working prototype along with a Report in
the pdf format and the Source code. The shortlisted teams will be invited to Shaastra for the finals.

 

Finals:

    The shortlisted teams will be given a new Problem Statement about a week before
Shaastra. The teams need to give a Presentation about their prototype during Shaastra.

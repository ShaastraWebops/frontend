Hindu Hackathon   

     Move over academic problems-get ready to tackle a real world coding problem at the Hindu Hackathon, a proud initiative of Shaastra 2015, in association with leading newspaper, The Hindu!

     And that's not all-selected participants would be offered an internship with The Hindu's digital team, where they can see their prototype taken live and applied in a real-world situation!

     The Problem Statement is grounded in some of the hottest topics of today: e-commerce, social networking, etc. All you have to do is showcase your coding expertise by preparing a working prototype of any one of the domains which has a real world application.

Onsite Prelims :

 Have you missed the schedule of Online Prelims and fell back in the competetion ?

 Don't worry . We are providing you yet another oppurtunity to participate in 

Triathalon-2015 . An Onsite Prelims will be held here at IITM on January,3 rd ,2015.

 

There will be common onsite prelims for Triathalon and Reverse Coding from 11.00am to 3.00pm.  Venue : Class Room Complex (CRC) ,Room No: 304 and 305.

It will be conducted in two sessions . One can participate only once. You have to register in the hospitality desk and they will inform you about the timing of your session. Registrations start from 8.30am.

  

Participants who got selected from both Online Prelims & Onsite Prelims will be allowed to participate in Finals.

So , keep your minds cracking  and fingers moving !

How Things Work

Timing: 12:00 to 13:30, 19th September

How Things Work quiz demands participants to come up with simple and perfectly logical explanations to the most bizarre phenomenon in Universe ranging from engineering to medical science, mathematics to theoretical physics. Immensely popular amongst the quizzing clan, HTW requires a blend of basic concepts of science, common sense and a strong gut feeling. HTW Quiz at Shaastra is not only popular but perhaps, one of the most unique quizzes in the country and well known for bringing out a number of ingenious and witty answers from the participants and has been witnessing huge turnout since its inception 10 years back growing each and every year.

On-spot registrations in teams of 3. Prizes worth Rs 3,000 up for grabs.

Project X

Timing: 15:30 to 16:30, 19th September

Project X is one of the colossal events at Shaastra in terms of the participation witnessed. The event is all about solving intriguing problems using basic day-to-day materials. Since the only requirements for the event are creativity, enthusiasm and craftsmanship, the event has been a big crowd puller with more than 1000 people participating each year.

On-spot registrations in teams of 3. Prizes worth Rs 3,000 up for grabs.

Robo-Oceana Workshop
Timing: 9:00 to 12:00, 19th September

Challenging waves, floating warriors of metal, perilous obstacles, match against time. Robo-Oceana is one of kind water robotics event that’s a delightful mix of technology, entertainment & creativity. This hands-on workshop is your chance to design your very own miniature marine robot. Students can participate as a team (maxi 4 members).

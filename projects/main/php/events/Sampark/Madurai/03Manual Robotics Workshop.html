Manual Robotics Workshop

Session 1: 8.30am - 11.00am, 18th October

Session 2: 4.00pm - 5.30pm, 18th October

Ever wondered how a robot works? This year, Shaastra gives you an opportunity to propel yourself into the world of robotics with a Manual Robotics workshop designed to give you an edge and inspire you to delve deeper by helping you understand the basics of robotics, mechanisms and components and then build your first remote-controlled robot. Learn the principles of differential drive, various clamping mechanisms.

Prerequisites?
None, just enough enthusiasm and you are good to go. Rest assured that you will get enough experience, so as to build various advanced clamping mechanisms using gears-structures, linkages, rack and pinion etc., limited only by your imagination. This workshop will be the foundation for your voyage into the world of robotics.

E-Certificates will be provided for participants.

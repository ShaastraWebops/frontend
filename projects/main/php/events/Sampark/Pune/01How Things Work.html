How Things Work
Timing: 3pm to 5pm, 23rd August

How Things Work quiz demands participants to come up with simple and perfectly logical explanations to the most bizarre phenomenon in Universe ranging from engineering to medical science, mathematics to theoretical physics. Immensely popular amongst the quizzing clan, HTW requires a blend of basic concepts of science, common sense and a strong gut feeling. HTW Quiz at Shaastra is not only popular but perhaps, one of the most unique quizzes in the country and has been witnessing huge turnout since its inception 10 years back growing each and every year.

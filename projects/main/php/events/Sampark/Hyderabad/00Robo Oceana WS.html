Robo-Oceana Workshop
Timing: 9:00 to 12:00, 19th October, 2014.

Challenging waves, floating warriors of metal, perilous obstacles, match against time. Robo-Oceana is one of kind water robotics event that’s a delightful mix of technology, entertainment & creativity. This hands-on workshop is your chance to design your very own miniature marine robot. Students can participate as a team (maxi 4 members).
Certificates will be provided to all participants of the workshop. 

This is a pre-registered event.

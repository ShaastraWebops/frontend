Boeing Aeromodelling Training Session  1:30 p.m. - 5 p.m.

 

Shaastra 2015 is back this edition with its unique set of competitions for budding Aeromodellers. If you are wondering how miniature RC aircrafts can be made with ease and want to blaze the sky with your aircraft, Shaastra provides you a short training session to ignite your flame for being an aeromodeller and design your dreams.

 

The session will consist of the following:

 

1. Informative Session: Shaastra team will conduct a basic informative session, telling you about the basics of aermodelling, the mechanics of it and how to approach basic fabrication and design of a RC aircraft (1 hour approx)  If you are an amateur modeller, this session is for you to be better. If you are a pro, this session is for you to know what other professional teams are up to :)

 

2. Questionarre: After the session, Shaastra team will take up doubts from the audience.

 

3. Mentoring Session: After the general session, Shaastra team will have a short session with the participants of the Boeing Aeromodelling Competition present there, where we will try to document and resolve their doubts in particular to the competition

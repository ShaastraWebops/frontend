The event will be in 3 steps.

 

1. WORKSHOP-  All the teams(max-3 members in one team) will be given a glimpse about FORENSICS. The participants will also be taught to identify fingerprints &  analyse blood samples.

2. LOGIC TEST- Mainly based on the workshop, we will take a short test of the teams  and select 5 teams who will be eligible for the final.

3. FINALS- There will be a  crime scene and the teams will have to crack the clues and  solve the crime.

 

                                                                                                                         Awards

                                            The first 2 teams with maximum points will get cash prize.

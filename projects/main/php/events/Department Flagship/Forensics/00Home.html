Introduction

When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth.

But would this truth suffice to identify the culprit, from a range of suspects, one of whom committed a murder at Shaastra 2015?

Get ready to comb through fingerprints and work thorugh piles of forensic evidence to find out who killed John Doe. If you are fascinated by murder and mystery, here's your chance. Fulfill your childhood dream and nab the cold-blooded killer, only at Forensics: Shaastra's only mystery solving event.

 

Event Format 

  • Teams of 3.
  • The event is an onspot event. No online rounds will be held.
  • Written prelims, 20 questions, 1 hour. Participants will be tested on basic forensics and mystery solving skills.
  • 6 teams make it to the finals.
  • A cleverly simulated crime scene awaits the finalists.

1. What will be the size of the team?

  • The minimum and maximum sizes of the team are restricted to 2 and 4. 


2. Is it mandatory to have civil engineering background to participate in this event?

  • Leader of the team has to have a civil engineering background, other team members can be from any engineering division.


3. Where do i register for the event?

  • This is a Pre-registered event. To participate in this event, the team needs to submit the TDP. Please refer to EVENT FORMAT tab.


4. Can i stay in the campus?

  • Yes, please refer to hospitality or accomodation page in the Shaastra website


5. Will the participation certificates be provided?

  • Certificates will be given only to the winner and the other finalists.

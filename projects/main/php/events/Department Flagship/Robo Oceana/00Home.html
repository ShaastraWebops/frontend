Introduction

 

Are you equally excited by the sound of waves and robots crashing into each other? If so, then RoboOceana is one event you should not miss. A delightful mix of technology, entertainment and creativity, this is one of the most breathtaking events at Shaastra. The problem statements ensure a tough yet entertaining competition, with huge prize money at stake.

So do you have what it takes to overcome your opponents while staying afloat and avoiding the killer waves? Compete to be proclaimed the most powerful battleship of Shaastra.

 

Event Format

Round-1: Prelims (TDP Submission)

In this round, teams will have to submit a Team Description Paper (TDP). You will have to provide your complete team details and answer a subjective questionnaire related to your design of the boat in the TDP.

Note: The format for submission will be released soon.


Round-2: Second Round

Shortlisted teams from the first round will be invited to IIT Madras during Shaastra 2014 . Second round will be a race between the selected teams on the game arena.

Note: A knockout format will be followed. 


Round-3: Third Round

Selected teams from second round will be going to the final round. Third round will be a polo match between teams where two robots of each team strive to target the goalpost with utmost accuracy to win the ultimate glory

Note: A knockout format will be followed.

Introduction

Are you equally excited by the sound of waves and robots crashing into each other? If so, then RoboOceana is one event you should not miss. Introduced for the first time during Shaastra 2013, RoboOceana was a resounding success. A delightful mix of technology, entertainment and creativity, this is one of the most breathtaking events at Shaastra. The problem statements ensure a tough yet entertaining competition, with huge prize money at stake.

So do you have what it takes to overcome your opponents while staying afloat and avoiding the killer waves? Compete to be proclaimed the most powerful battleship of Shaastra.

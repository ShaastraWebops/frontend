Frequently Asked Questions

1. What will be the size of the team?

  • The maximum size of the team is restricted to 6 members.


2. Do all members need to be from the same college?

  • No. Team members can be from different colleges.


3. Is there any deadline for submission?

  • The TDP’s should be uploaded before 30th October,2014.


4. I am a student from a different stream and have no prior robotics experience. Can I participate?

  • Yes, you can. A detailed tutorial for the competition is also available which will be released along with the problem statement.

Frequently Asked Questions

 

Q1 : Who can register and what is the fee for registration ?

Ans : Anyone who has been with RC cars for more than an year can register and registration is free.

 

Q2 : How many members can a team have ?

Ans : Maximum number of participants allowed in a team is 5.

 

Q3 : We have RC car powered by battery, can we participate ?

Ans : No or any other propulsion means is prohibited.

 

Q4 : Do we need to bring fuel with us ?

Ans : Yes , fuel will also be sold on the event site . But if you are willing to bring your own fuel then make sure that it does not exceed 20% Nitro-methane by volume (please mention whether you're going to bring your own fuel or not in your TDP).

 

Q5 : Will participation certificates be provided?

Ans : Only winner and finalist certificates will be given.

 

Q6: When is the event going to held and where? 

Ans: Fire N ICE will happen during Shaastra, right from Day 1. Only registered teams can participate.

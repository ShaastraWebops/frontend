FAQ

1. What is the maximum number of people in a team?
A: Maximum of 5 people in a team are allowed.

2. Is it necessary that all the team members should be from the same college?
A: No, It is not mandatory that all the team members should be from the same college.

3. What is the meaning of lifting the chassis?
A: Chassis is the base frame of robot where all the basic mechanisms are mounted, teams need to lift their chassis for entering into zone B or can avoid chassis lifting by following the rule 5.3.3

4. Can any part of robot detach from itself?
A: No, any kind of separation or detaching things from the robot is not allowed

5. Is the hurdle fixed to the arena?
A: Yes, it is fixed to the arena and cannot be moved.

6. Can we modify our TDP after submitting once?
A: Yes, you can modify your TDP and upload the modified file by replacing the old file as long as you are submitting before the deadline. 

7. Are there any prerequisites to register for this event?

A: No.

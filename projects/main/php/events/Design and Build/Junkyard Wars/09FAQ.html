FAQ

1.Is it required to have mechanical engineering background to participate in this event?

A.No, everyone can participate on this event.There is no restriction on branch of the student.


2.Will the questions for first round and second round be tough?

A.Questions for first round will be related to day to day life physics and simple mechanics. Second round will be slightly tougher than the first round as it will contain questions related to designs.

3.Can I play with only 3 members in a team?

A.Yes,but it would be difficult to complete the problem statement once you become a finalist for the last round.

4.Where do I register for the event?

A.The teams are expected to turn up on time for the first round and register on the spot near the QMS- Hospitality desk. There is no pre-registration required for this event.

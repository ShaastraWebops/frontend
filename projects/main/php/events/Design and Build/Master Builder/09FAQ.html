FAQ

Q.1 What will be the size of the team?
A.  The minimum and maximum sizes of the team are restricted to 2 and 4. 

Q.2 Is it mandatory to have civil engineering background to participate in this event?
A.  Leader of the team has to have a civil engineering background, other team members can be from any engineering division.

Q.3 Where do I register for the event?
A. This is a Pre-registered event. To participate in this event, the team needs to submit the TDP. Please refer to EVENT FORMAT.

Q.4 Can i stay in the campus?
A.  Yes, please refer to hospitality section.

Q.5 Will the participation certificates be provided?
A.  Certificates will be given only to the winner and the other finalists.

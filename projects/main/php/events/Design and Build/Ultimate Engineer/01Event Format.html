Event Format

Team Structure:

Teams can contain a maximum of 4 students . Participants need not be from same institution but from different departments is recommended. Everyone should present their College ID card during event.

Online  Prelims:

Written round with online registration.

  • Each Team  need to register online and submit their TDP before deadline for participating in this event.
  • First 3 teams (subject to change) from the online prelims will be selected for the second round which going happen in IIT MADRAS during SHAASTRA.
  • The questions will be of objective.
  • Dates for online prelims to be released soon.

 

Offline Prelims(Sampark):

Written round with on the spot registration.

  • We will be coming to your city and your college.For more details click here.
  • One team from each city will be selected for the second round.
  • The questions will be of both objective and descriptive types

Round 1:Prelims:-

Written round with on the spot registration

  • This round will be held in IIT MADRAS during shaastra.
  • The top 20 teams (subject to change) from the prelims will be selected for the second round.
  • The questions will be of both objective and descriptive types.

NOTE:

For both Online, Offline prelims The written test, spanning about 75-90 minutes, will test the participants on their understanding of the most fundamental science and engineering concepts pertaining to all the branches, complimented by their quantitative and reasoning abilities.

The test does not test any core engineering concepts. Courses done in high school and the 1st year of engineering will more than sufficient for the participants to take up the test.

 

Round-2:

A hands-on cum written round

  • Shortlisted teams from Online prelims, Offline prelims and First Round will be invited to a more engrossing engineering battle in the second round. This round comprises two groups each consisting of 3-5 broader engineering themes among which the teams need to choose one in each group and accomplish the problem statements pertaining to the themes they have chosen within the prescribed time limit.
  • The event tests both the analytical and creative ability of the students as well as their levels of application of engineering concepts to solve real life problems.

 

Note: All the requisite materials and other resources for brainstorming will be provided to the participants during the conduct of the event.

FAQ

Q1 :What are the maximum and minimum weights of the robot?
Ans :The maximum allowed weight of the robot inside the arena is 25kg and minimum weight is 7kg.
Ex: If team A is using external batteries, then only the weight of the robot (excluding the batteries)will be measured. However, if Team B is placing batteries on-board, then the maximum allowed weight(with batteries) is 25kg and minimum weight(excluding batteries) is 7kg.Also, the weight of the controller and the wires will not be measured.

Q2 : What is the maximum permitted potential difference?
Ans The maximum permitted potential difference is 40V DC.

Q3: For how long will the crusher and lifter be active ?
Ans: The crusher can be active for 5s, and the lifter can be active for a maximum of 5s. NOTE: active means maximum time upto which they can be operational per usage


Q4 : What are Offensive and Defensive weapons?
Ans : The weapons or mechanisms used for attacking the opponent's robot are Offensive weapons.and similarly weapons or mechanisms used for defending the robot from opponent's robot are called Defensive weapons.

Q5: How are points for staying in sumo ring awarded?
Ans: Suppose a robot stays for 10sec and 20 sec in the sumo ring with an interval between these times, the total points awarded to the team will be (10*10)+(20*20)=500.
Q6: Are we allowed to use DC motors for operating the Robots?
Ans : Yes, you can.

Q7: Will participation certificates be provided?
Ans : Only winner and finalists certificates will be given.

Q8: Is there any prerequisite to register for the event?
Ans: No

Contraptions

 

"For every complex problem there is an answer that is clear, simple and wrong"  - H.L. Mencken

Then why do simple at all??

For all those who think out of the box, this event is definitely meant for you. A truly unique hands-on event at Shaastra 2015, Contraptions will make your head spin, trying to catch-up with all the action going on.

Contraptions are deliberately over-engineered or overdone machines that perform a very simple task in a very complex fashion, usually including a chain reaction.

It's the only event of its kind which tests a wide range of skills of the team such as robotics, disaster proof construction, the power to innovate and an impeccable implementation of ideas - resulting in spectacular masterpieces that entertain and execute!

 

As the years have gone by, we humans have made magnanimous progress in science and technology , enabling us to raise the standard of our living. These inventions have been in tandem with the times, rolling out and improvising on antecedent discoveries, much like a huge contraption on a global scale. Hence the theme for this year is going to be 'Progress of Mankind', wherein the participants follow in the footsteps of great Scientists through time.

 

While most other events seek to simplify, Contraptions requires you to enthrall the judges and the audience with your ability to complicate the simplest of actions with an infinite sequence of steps! A event where people battle against each other and themselves, Contraptions has a history of showcasing raw brilliance and has become one of the most popular event of the masses at Shaastra.

 

With attractive prize money, industrial involvement and enormous audiences watching you compete, there is a lot at stake for you.

Can you go the distance?

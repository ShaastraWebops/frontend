Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 : Who can register and what is the fee for registration ?

Ans : Anyone who has been with RC cars for more than an year can register and registration is free.

 

Q2 : How many members can a team have ?

Ans : Maximum number of participants allowed in a team is 5.

 

Q3 : We have RC car powered by battery, can we participate ?

Ans : No or any other prpulsion means is prohibited.

 

Q4 : Do we need to bring fuel with us ?

Ans : Yes , fuel will also be sold on the event site . But if you are willing to bring your own fuel then make sure that it does not exceed 20% Nitro-methane by volume (please mention whether your going to bring your own fuel or not in your TDP)

 

Q5 : Will participation certificates be provided?

Ans : Only winner and finalist certificates will be given.

 

Q6: Could readymade parts be directly installed on the car?

Ans:No, the modules which have been mentioned in the Problem statement have to been manufactured by the team themselves, other modules can be bought.

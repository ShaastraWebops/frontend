ARM Workshop

Brought to you by Shaastra 2015 in association with STEPS Knowledge Services Pvt ltd

 

Interested in hands-on experience in designing and programming embedded system based projects? Here is your chance!

The one day hands-on workshop covers the architecture of ARM Cortex M4 Microcontroller from Texas Instruments, data, program memory, peripheral organization and its special features. The peripherals include I/O Ports, Timers, PWM, A/D Converters, Universal Serial Interfaces and Comparators. The course is structured and tailored to meet the need of the engineering students to get a very good exposure of ARM Cortex M4 Controllers which can be very well extrapolated to any other platform. Typical applications could be battery powered sensor signal acquisition, processing and serial transmission to a host system.

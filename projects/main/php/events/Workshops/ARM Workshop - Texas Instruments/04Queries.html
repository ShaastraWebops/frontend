Frequently Asked Questions

Attention: The schedule for workshops has been released. Find it here.  Please go through the schedule and register for workshops that do not clash.

Q1) Is there any prerequisite knowledge needed for the workshop?

A) Understanding basic C programming is required.

 

Q2) Do I really need a complete understanding of the microprocessor architecture?

A) Basic Understanding would be good, but not really required. Would be taught during the workshop. 

 

Q3) Do I need to bring any tools for the workshop?

A)Laptops are required and a kit comprising of launch pad will be given to the team.

 

Q4) Do we get certificates for participation in the workshop?

A)Yes, Everyone participating in the workshop will receive a certificate.

 

Q5) Can we register on-the-spot for this workshop?

A)No. Online Pre-Registration is required.

 

Q6) What documents I need to carry for the workshop?

A) Photo ID Card from a recognized institution/government organization.

Rubik's Cube Workshop

Intrigued by how people can solve Rubik's Cubes at mind-boggling speeds without even batting an eyelid? 

Felt intimidated by the multiple colours that never seem to align the way you want them to? 

Then here is an opportunity for you to learn to solve the six-faced puzzle that till date remains a classic for all ages. The best part? No Pre-Registration Required! Walk in at the venue and join us in learning how to approach and tackle this multiple faced conundrum. 

The Rubik’s cube workshop includes demonstrations of 3x3, 4x4, 5x5 solves and many more interesting demos made for your understanding. This workshop shall leave you with a fascinating habit that would surely last a lifetime and the hunger to crack puzzles like a pro!

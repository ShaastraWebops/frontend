Traditional to Modern Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is an art, Engineers and scientific tools brings it to life!

Today in the competitive world, an extra edge can make you stand out. In India, we are still years behind the world’s latest developments, and we can change this with time. Lets modernize our civil engineering industry.

This workshop is about introducing you to some basics of software in the fields of Transportation and Analytics, and Project Management; one of the most crucial fields of Civil Engineering. Working with right software can make your hard work- smart work. The more curious you are, the more you can bring out of these tools. It is an ever learning journey which begins with this workshop. And this workshop will definitely offer you a different perspective of civil engineering, the modern way of construction.

                                             Frequently  Asked Questions

 

​Q) Who all can take part in this Workshop?​

A)  Students from any college or university can take part in this workshop.

 

Q) What documents I need to carry for the workshop?

A) Photo ID Card from a recognized institution/government organization.

 

Q) What is the team limit ? Should all the team members be from same college?

A)  Maximum team limit is 5,all the team members need not be from same college.

 

Q) What all do we have to bring from home  for workshop?

A)  All the materials needed for the workshop will be provided,so you dont have to  bring anything from home.              

    

Q)  Is there spot registration for the Workshop?

A)  No, there is no spot registration,you have to register online by submitting the TDP.

 

Q) What is TDP? Is it compulsory to submit it.

A) TDP is Team Description paper.It is compulsory to submit it as it is the first selection criteria for the  event

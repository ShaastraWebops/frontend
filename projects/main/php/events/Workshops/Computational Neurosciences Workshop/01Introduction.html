Computational Neurosciences Workshop

At present there seems nothing more exciting than to know how the human brain works. Brain is the most complex, sophisticated, and powerful information-processing device known. Computational neuroscience is a multidisciplinary endeavour pooling together methods from computer science, mathematics and physics to complement experimental research in neuroscience, and to address the analysis of complex data sets generated by the brain.

This Shaastra, we bring you a workshop on Computational Neuroscience using MATLAB, to be conducted by Research Scholars of IIT Madras, to expose you to the computational principles governing various aspects of vision and sensory-motor control.

Q1. What is the size of a team? How can I register for this workshop.?

A1. The team size will be announced as soon as registrations open. The details regarding the registration will be mentioned in the "Registration" tab.

 

Q2. Should the team consist of members from the same college? Can people who are not students participate in the workshop?

A2. No. Any set of students can form a team. Each student must present  a valid ID from a recognized educational institution.This workshop is open to people from all walks of life,anyone with tech enthusiasm can participate in the workshop. But all non-students must have a valid personal ID.

 

Q3. What are the prerequisites to attend the workshop?

A3. Absolutely nothing. This is a beginner's workshop, so we will be starting from scratch. 

 

Q4. What materials should I bring for the workshop?

A4. You need not bring anything for this workshop. All tools, materials and equipment required will be provided by us.

 

Q5. Will I get accommodation during Shaastra for the workshop ?

A5. You can apply for accommodation at the hospitality department of Shaastra. It will be on a first come first serve basis.

 

Q6. Does participating in this workshop mean I will not be able to participate in Shaastra?

A6. No. You will be spending a lot of your time in the workshop, but this does not prevent you from participating in any other events.Time slots will be put up soon, and you can participate as long as you avoid a clash.

 

Q7. How do I benefit from this workshop?

A7. This workshop provides a unique opportunity to learn basic working of a robot . You will be learning the basics of hydrodynamics and the functioning of the various components used in the building of the robot.

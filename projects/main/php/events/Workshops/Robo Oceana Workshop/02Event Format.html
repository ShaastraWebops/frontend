Round 1:

  • Register yourselves in teams comprising of 3 members along with the contact details of all the participants in the registration portal. Registration will begin soon.
  • Each team will have to fill up an online questionnaire (TDP) which will be used to shortlist the total number of participating teams. 
  • Each team has to pay an amount of Rs.2000 to be a part of this workshop. The methods of transaction will be put up soon.  
  • The workshop registration will be open to limited number of teams.

 

Round 2:

  • Short-listed teams will be invited to IIT Madras during Shaastra 2015, to be held from 3rd to 6th January for attending the workshop.
  • The workshop will be conducted on one of the four days of Shaastra for 3-4 hours. 
  • Each group will be provided with an instruction manual and a take away robotics kit For each workshop. 
  • For workshop, under the guidance of experts, the participants will have hands on experience in making a Surface Boat.
  • Participants are expected to make the bots at the venue itself in the given time.
  • All the participants will be given Certificate of Participation by Shaastra-2015.

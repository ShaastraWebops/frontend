The Mini Spad is a RC (radio controlled) Airplane, remote controlled by a pilot on ground. The control surfaces in the plane such as elevator, aileron and rudder controls pitch, roll and yaw movement respectively.Thrust is provided by the horizontal propeller mounted to the tip of the airplane. 

 

This fascinating vehicle has been a keen part of interest of many RC fliers. Flying RC airplane for hobby has been growing worldwide with the advent of more efficient motors, lighter and powerful batteries and less expensive radios.

Team Composition:
The participants will be divided into teams, each comprising of 3 to 5 members.  The composition of the team may include members of either same college or different colleges.

Workshop Details:
The workshop will take place for 4 to 5 hours. Under the able guidance of experts, knowledge of design, fabrication and assembly of all parts of the glider and their significance in order to understand the dynamics of airplane would be imparted.
Each group would be provided with a take away balsa wood kit. Participants are expected to make the glider at the venue itself in a given time .

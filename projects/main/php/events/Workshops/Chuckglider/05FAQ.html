Q1. Do all members of a team need to be from the same college?

A1. No. Students from any college or school can form a team of maximum 4 members.

 

Q2. What are the prerequisites to attend the workshop?

A2. There are no prerequisites for this workshop except the enthusiasm to participate.

 

Q3. Will the materials for the workshop be provided?

A3. Yes, all required materials for the workshop will be provided.

 

Q4. Will I get accommodation during Shaastra for the workshop?

A4. You can apply for accommodation at the Hospitality Department of Shaastra. It will be on first come first serve basis.

 

Q5. Will I be able to attend other events at Shaastra?

A5. Yes, there will be plenty of time for attending other events. The exact timing of the workshop will be updated soon.

Q) Do the participants have to bring their own laptops?

A) Yes.

Note:The links to the softwares that we'll be using are provided in the resources tab.

Q) Is there a limit on the number of participants?

A) Yes. The number of participants allowed will be announced soon. On spot, off-line registration is also possible for a limited number of people, depending on availability.

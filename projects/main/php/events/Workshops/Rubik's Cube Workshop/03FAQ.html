Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1 : Do we need to get our own cubes?
A: No.Cubes can be borrowed for the workshop.

Q2: Will cubes be sold at the venue? 

A: Yes, cubes will be available for participants who are interested in buying and mastering  the cube solving techniques.

Q3: Are registrations limited to a certain number of teams? 

A: Yes, the registrations will close after the limit is reached. The finalised participants will be notified by a mail.

Q4: Do we need to pay for this workshop? 

A: No, it is absolutely free of cost.


 

Note :   Shaastra ID is a must to participate.

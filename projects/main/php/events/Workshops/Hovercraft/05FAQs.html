•  Who can register for the workshop?

 Students from any college/university with a valid student ID can register for the workshop.

 

  • Is the workshop only for students?

The workshop is not necessarily restricted to students. Anybody from any age group with tech enthusiasm can register for the workshop. But the non-students should have a valid Identity proof.

 

  • What are the pre-requisites to attend the workshop?

This is a beginner's workshop. So everything will be taught from scratch at the workshop. So, no prerequisites are needed other than enthusiasm and curiosity to learn.

 

  • How should we register for the workshop?

 Registration for the workshop will be open soon. The mode of registration will be online. Every team will be charged a registration fee that involves the expenses of the workshop kit. Also, every team is supposed to submit a Team Description Paper(TDP) at the time of registration. The format for the Team Description Paper(TDP) will be released soon. 

 

  • What is the team limit? Should all of them be from same institute?

 The team can involve a maximum of 5 members and a minimum of 3 members. Students from different institutes can form a team and participate.

 

  • Do we need to bring any tools for the workshop?

 Complete kit needed for making the hovercraft will be provided ,so no need to bring anything.

 

  • Will i get accomodation during Shaastra for the workshop?

 You can apply for accommodation at the hospitality department of Shaastra. It will be on a first come first serve basis.

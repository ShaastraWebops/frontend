  • This workshop will be conducted over 2 days, from Day 2 (4 Jan'15) to Day 3 (5 Jan'15). 
  • The duration of each session would be 7 hours.
  • A break will be provided.
  • The participant teams will receive a kit of their own to work with during the workshop.

The workshop will begin with a small introduction about the hovercraft and its working principles with basics on electronics.. Our team will be there with you always to help you build the perfect hovercraft. In the meantime, we will help you gain clarity about the design of the hovercraft and the components used in building it. By the end of the workshop, participating teams will have an Line following Hovercraft which they can take home!.Along with that you will be taught to convert it to RC(Remote Controlled). But most importantly, participants will witness how application of simple science can make things work.

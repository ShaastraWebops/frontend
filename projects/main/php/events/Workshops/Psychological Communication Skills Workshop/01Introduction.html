Introduction

This workshop is brought to you by Shaastra 2015 in association with School Of Intelligence, Indore (SOI). SOI is giving you a workshop on the psychological aspect of communicating, demonstrated by NLP (Neuro-linguistic programming)  practitioners.

In general when one person expresses himself, and the second person understands him, a communication between the two of them is established. But what if that person is not even willing to listen to you? What if you find it difficult to reason with them? What if a "talk" on an issue turns into a "debate" everytime? The solutions to these problems are contained in understanding the psychology of communication. 

If you want to improve your communication skills and develop a confident exterior, do turn up for the Psychological Communication Skills Workshop and let your hidden creativty flow!

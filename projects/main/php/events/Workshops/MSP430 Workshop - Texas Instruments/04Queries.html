Frequently Asked Questions

Attention: The schedule for workshops has been released. Find it here.  Please go through the schedule and register for workshops that do not clash.

Q1) Is there any prerequisite knowledge needed for the workshop?

A) Understanding of basic C Programming required. Understanding of Microprocessor Architecture is desired, but optional.

 

Q2) Do I need to bring any tools for the workshop?

A)Laptops are required and a kit comprising of launch pad will be given to the team.

 

Q3) Can we register on-the-spot for this workshop?

A)No. Online Pre-Registration is required.

 

Q4) What documents I need to carry for the workshop?

A) Photo ID Card from a recognized institution/government organization.

 

Q5) Can we take the kits back home?

The launchpad kit can be retained by the teams after the workshop.

 

In case of any further queries or clarifications, feel free to mail us at associated_workshops@shaastra.org

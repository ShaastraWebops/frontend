Steps for taking MCMF test:

Through Online Payment:

  • Use the link to register.
  • After registration use the link to add MCMF to the cart.
  • Use the Discount coupon code (SHAASTRA2015) to redeem 30% discount.
  • On successful payment, you will get instant access to the test (through email with an access code).
  • After the test is completed, an analysis report will be generated in the form of PDF.
  • Download and save the PDF.
  • Get the printout of the report to attend the workshop .
  • Registrations are open during all the days of Shaastra both offline and online.

 

Through Offline Payment:

  • Visit the stall in SHAASTRA and collect the coupon code by paying the amount.
  • Get 30% discount while paying the amount.

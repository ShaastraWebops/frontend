Ravi Venkatesan

One of India’s most respected CEO’s, Ravi Venkatesan is also a social entrepreneur, mentor and author of the book Conquering the Chaos : Win in India, Win Everywhere – an insider’s view to winning in emerging markets. This former Chairman of Microsoft India and Cummins India is a passionate advocate of India’s potential. Ravi persuasively talks about business, innovation, globalization, social entrepreneurship, and developing leaders for a better world.

As an industry leader, Ravi has always been a strong proponent of a philosophy of “doing well by doing good.” With successful initiatives like Project Shiksha at Microsoft India and the all-women engineering college at Cummins India, Ravi has walked the talk on creating shared value.

Ravi’s book, Conquering the Chaos: Win in India, Win Everywhere, has been recently launched. In the book and at various forums, Ravi uses a personal and emotional connect through storytelling.

Ravi has founded Social Venture Partners, a network of philanthropists, and is a director on the boards of Infosys and AB Volvo. He is also a member of the Advisory Boards of Harvard Business School, Bunge Ltd. and a Fellow of the Center for Higher Ambition Leadership in Boston.

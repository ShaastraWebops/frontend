TI University Programme Workshops

Date: 23 and 24 August

Time: 9:30 am to 5:00 pm

Topic: " Introduction to MSP430 Microcontrollers "

Venue: Electrical Sciences Block (ESB)

About the Workshop:

Workshops by the TI University Programme have been loved by students all over India. So,the Shaastra 2015 Events team decided to organize an exciting workshop under the TI (Texas Instruments) University Programme. This free workshop conducted by Edgate Technologies, a partner company of the TI University Programme in collaboration with the CFI Electronics Club and the Electrical Engineering Department.

The two day long workshop was on the topic ' Introduction to MSP430 Microcontrollers '. Conducted by professionals, the workshop was a hands-on workshop with students programming and making circuits with the MSP430 micro-controller.

Overwhelming Response:

We got an overwhelming response for the workshop with 250 students registering for the workshop which could accommodate only 80 students. Hence, we have decided to conduct a similar workshop in the month of October. Whether you are a beginner with electronics, or an electronics enthusiast, this workshop is a must-attend for you. Details about this will be informed to you soon.

Introduction to Embedded Systems Workshop

Date: 7th and 10th November

Time: 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm

Topic: " Hands-On Session with the Arduino "

Venue: Electrical Sciences Block (ESB)

About the Workshop:

Most of us have worked with electronics and embedded systems and felt the need for learning more about them. Hence, we planned a fully hands-on workshop on the embedded systems, being conducted by professionals. This free workshop was conducted by Ingenstech Pvt. Ltd. in collaboration with the CFI Electronics Club and the Electrical Engineering Department.

The two day long workshop was a hands-on session on the Arduino. Conducted by professionals, the workshop was very successful with students making circuits and programming on the Arduino.

As a follow up to this workshop, we are planning to conduct a 5-day hands-on workshop on the Arduino starting from 29th November. Details of this will be informed to you soon. Whether you are a beginner with electronics, or an electronics enthusiast, this workshop is a must-attend for you.

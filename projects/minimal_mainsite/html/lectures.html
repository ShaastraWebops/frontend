LECTURES

One of the highlights of Shaastra 2014, was an ambitious initiative to bring the veterans in all fields of science and technology under one roof for interacting with the students and professors of IIT Madras and the participants of Shaastra. 16 Speakers, 6000 unique students over 4 days at IIT Madras was an enticing experience for many. From technocrats to students, from free software activists to founders of the most dynamic NGOs in India, Shaastra had it all.

Over the years, Shaastra has evolved to become the platform for exchange of ideas that drive the world of science and technology. Lectures and Exhibitions are the most effective ways of interaction for the stellars with beginners in every field. We have been hosts to several nobel laureates and global thought leaders whom the world hails as visionaries. Their interaction with motivated students is helping the growth of the next generation of leaders.

Speakers love interacting with world renowned faculty of this premier institute of technology in India, apart from visiting IIT Madras Research Park which incubates hi-tech startups and provides technical consultancy to tech giants in the market. Apart from that, there is heritage and culture to explore in Chennai, the capital city of Tamil Nadu, ranging from Carnatic Music concerts to various dance forms indigenous to India.

RICHARD STALLMAN

Richard Stallman, has been an ardent champion of free and open source software through GPL license. Stallman has been an exceptional developer and has received numerous awards, MacArthur Fellowship, EFF Pioneer award, ACM Grace Murray Hopper award, to name a few.

STEPHEN WOLFRAM

Stephen Wolfram, is the chief designer and developer of the world renowned software Mathematica and WolframAlpha Computational knowledge engine. He is also an awardee of the MacArthur Fellowships, has over 30,000 citations and has an ErdOs number of 2.

VINOD KHOSLA

Vinod Khosla, a famous Indian-American entrepreneur and a venture capitalist, is the co-founder of SUN Microsystems, and the founder of Khosla Ventures. He is an advocate of clean energy and technology. In addition, Khosla is a member of the board of trustees of the Blum center for developing economies at UC, Berkeley, which is focussed on finding solutions to address the crisis of extreme poverty and disease in the developing world.

JOHN R. ADLER

John R. Adler is an American neurosurgeon. He is the inventor of the CyberKnife, and image guided radiosurgical robotic instrument. Adler holds 9 United States patents and has authored over 180 peer-reviewed articles and book chapters. Adler is also the founder of Cureus.com, a web-based peer-reviewed medical journal.

ANANT AGARWAL

Anant Agarwal, is a computer architecture researcher. He is an EECS professor at MIT and the president of edX, a massive open online course platform. Besides, hs is also the founder and CTO of Tilera, a semiconductor company. He is an alumnus of IIT Madras, and holds a PhD in electrical engineering from Stanford University.

SRINIVAS VARDHAN

Srinivas Vardhan FRS, is an Indian-American mathematician who is known for his fundamental contributions to probability theory and in particular for creating a unified theory of large deviations. Vardhan has been the recipient of numerous awards, including the National Medal of Science(2010), the Padma Bhushan(2008) and the Abel Prize(2007).

JOHN C. MATHER

John C. Mather is an American astrophysicist, cosmologist and Nobel Prize in Physics (2006) Laureate for his work in the COBE. In 2007, Mather was listed among Time magazine's 100 Most Influential people in the world. He was also awarded the by American Academy of Arts(1996), Sciences Rumforn Prize and the Robinson Prize in Cosmology(2008).

RODDAM NARASIMHA

Roddam Narasimha FRS, is a famousIndian aerospace scientist and fluid dynamist. He has been the Director of NAL and the Chairman of Engineering Mechanics Unit at INCASR. He was instrumental in establishing a major parallel computing initiative in the country. He is the recipient of the Bhatnagar prize, the Gujarmal Modi award and the Padma Bhushan.
